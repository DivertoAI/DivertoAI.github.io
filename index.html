FAQ

Detailing Answers

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our packages are designed for 1 to 5 years depending on selected layers and maintenance schedule.

Can bikes get the same coating quality as cars?

Yes. We run separate prep and application workflows optimized for tanks, fairings, and exposed components.

How soon can I wash my vehicle after coating?

Avoid chemical wash for the first 7 days. We provide a safe post-coating care guide after delivery.

Do you provide pickup and drop?

Pickup and drop is available in selected service zones for premium packages. Confirm while booking.
